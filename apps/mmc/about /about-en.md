###MMC: My Multi Counter  

Create multiple counters for various purposes  

Sometimes all you need is to keep a number in mind!  
 
<b>MMC is the answer!</b>   

Create a counter now and start counting! You can add or subtract it and every daily operation is recorded in history.  
  
<b>Categories</b>  
  
You can separate your counters into different categories to organize them appropriately for some purpose.  

<b>Freemium / PRO</b>  

MMC is a free app, but you also have the option to unlock even more features by activating the PRO package.  
  
★ Create as many categories you want  
★ Backup and restore your categories  
★ Use dark mode
